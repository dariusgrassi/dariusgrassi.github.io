Darius Grassi

I am a doctoral student in UT Austin's Department of Computer Science. My research is advised by Professors Daehyeok Kim and Aditya Akella. I am broadly interested in problems related to computer networking, operating systems, and computer architecture. My current research is focused on systems problems for future programmable NIC architectures.

Previously, I graduated with a BSc in computer science from UIC, where I was mentored by Professor Brent Stephens. I also interned at the Open Networking Foundation.
